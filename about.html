About Me

I've worked in the fields of counselling, therapy, and child protection since 2007. After graduating with a Bachelor of Behavioural Science (Psychology) degree I began my career working in Child Protection Services. I helped parents and families create safe environments in their homes and work through complex relational problems.

Since completing a Master of Mental Health (Family Therapy) degree, I've been working primarily with individuals, couples and families as a counsellor and psychotherapist. I also work with large family groups to come together and resolve difficulties that impact children.

When meeting with clients I bring warmth, empathy and intuition along with my extensive experience of working with children, individuals, couples, parents, and families.
